Born and raised in the lush hills of Kandy, Sri Lanka, I grew up curious about patterns—both in math and in life. The misty hills and vibrant streets sparked my imagination.

As a child, I spent hours sketching patterns, watching the way sunlight danced through the trees, and noticing small mathematical patterns everywhere.

My passion for numbers led me to pursue higher education abroad. During my Ph.D., I delved into quantum topology, knot theory, and the elegance of abstract mathematics, presenting my work at conferences and publishing papers along the way.

When not immersed in equations, I explore the world through art and nature. Painting, photography, and hiking allow me to slow down, notice details, and stay inspired.
